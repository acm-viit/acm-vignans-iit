Computer Awareness Program

  • ACTIVITY: Visakhapatnam
  • STARTED ON: 7 Feb, 2021 10:00 AM IST
  • ENDED ON: Currently in Process, 2021

Developing a constructive school environment is critical to any school these days, so developing technical skills in students to adapt themselves to real-world technologies gives them motivation, confidence and the ability to learn and compete with the present world. So we, ACM VIIT made an initiative to nurture the students of ZPH High School Kanithi, Vadlapudi by teaching them topics such as Basics of Computers, Types of Computers, Applications of Computers, Outline of OS, and RTOS. Responding is the primary way to express their interest in it, the way the students engaged and interacted with us on the very first session on 7th February motivated us to make it a practice to teach them every Saturday till now, to boost interest in every student we gifted them small gifts to them who answered the best to the questions and problems posed. This event boosted up our responsibility towards building a better future for India.
